Hi. I'm Brandon

Professional Summary

My name is Brandon Perez, and I am a student with a Computer Science and Applied Mathematics background with a deep interest in solving problems and creating meaningful solutions through technology. I am fascinated by the process of transforming complex ideas into practical systems, whether that means developing software that simplifies everyday tasks, designing data-driven applications, or experimenting with new tools to expand my skill set. What excites me most about technology is its potential to connect people, streamline processes, and inspire innovation.

Collaboration and Leadership

Collaboration is at the center of how I approach both learning and professional work. I thrive in environments where creativity and teamwork drive results, and I enjoy bringing people together to turn ideas into reality. My background in leadership has helped me understand the value of communication, accountability, understanding people at a deeper level, and fostering a team culture that empowers everyone to contribute. These qualities guide how I hope to make an impact.

Hands-on Experience

Beyond the classroom, I have explored these interests through hands-on projects. Most recently, I developed a point-of-sale system that integrates a recommendation feature and provides data reports on customer orders. This project challenged me to consider how businesses use technology to manage operations and how predictive insights can improve decision-making.

Life Outside of Technology

Outside of coding, I am committed to maintaining balance and perspective. I enjoy traveling to new places, exercising, cooking, and spending quality time with loved ones. These experiences help me grow personally and often spark new ways of thinking that I carry into my professional work.

Looking Ahead

Ultimately, I aim to bring together my technical skills, collaborative mindset, and passion for continuous learning to contribute to teams where connection, innovation, and impact are at the forefront.

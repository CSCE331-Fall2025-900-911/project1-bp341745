Technical Background

I have a strong technical foundation in software development, with hands-on experience in building full-stack applications using technologies such as React, Node.js/Express, and PostgreSQL. My projects have involved creating intuitive user interfaces with modern frontend frameworks, implementing secure backend systems with authentication and role management, and managing data flow across relational and NoSQL databases. I've also worked with deployment practices, RESTful APIs, and cloud-based tools that emphasize both functionality and user experience.

My interests

I'm particularly interested in full-stack development where I can bring together frontend design and backend logic into cohesive solutions. I enjoy UI design for its creative problem-solving aspect and the opportunity to craft seamless user experiences. As the future of enhanced AI and quantum computing grows, I want to learn more about opportunities on how I can contribute to meaningful projects.
